## Overview

The base commission for **OT Swap** has been updated to **1%**, establishing a balanced fee structure across all protocol participants.

## Fee Distribution

| Destination | Share | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Project Reserve | 0.50% | Funds project development and operations |
| LP Provider | 0.25% | Rewards liquidity providers for their contribution |
| Areal Treasury | 0.25% | Supports long-term protocol sustainability |

## Rationale

The updated fee structure ensures:

- **Fair compensation** for liquidity providers who enable efficient swaps
- **Sustainable funding** for individual project reserves
- **Protocol-level revenue** to support Areal Treasury and governance initiatives

## Impact

This change applies to all new OT Swap transactions. Existing positions are not affected. The total **1% fee** is automatically split at the smart contract level during each swap execution.
